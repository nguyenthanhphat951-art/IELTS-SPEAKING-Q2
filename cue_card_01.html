🎤 Sample Speech
✅ Band 7+ Sample Speech (1.5–2 minutes)

I would like to talk about a birthday cake I received from my closest friends about two years ago. It was my twenty-second birthday, and I had no idea they were planning anything special. They had secretly arranged a small gathering at a cafe near our university campus. When I arrived, thinking we were just meeting for coffee, they brought out this stunning three-tiered chocolate cake decorated with edible flowers and my name written in gold icing. I was completely taken aback. What made it truly special was not just the appearance - though it was genuinely beautiful - but the fact that my friends had ordered it from a bakery that specialises in custom cakes, and they had included a small photo of all of us on the top tier. The cake itself was a rich dark chocolate sponge with raspberry cream filling, which happens to be my absolute favourite combination. As they sang happy birthday and I blew out the candles, I felt genuinely overwhelmed with gratitude. Looking back on it now, it is one of those moments I will always treasure - not because of the cake itself, but because of the love and effort my friends put into making me feel special. It reminded me of how fortunate I am to have such thoughtful people in my life.
