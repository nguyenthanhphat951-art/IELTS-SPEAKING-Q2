🎤 Sample Speech
✅ Band 7+ Sample Speech (1.5–2 minutes)

I would like to describe a time I got up particularly early about a year ago, when I joined a group of friends for a sunrise hike up a mountain near our city. We had planned the trip for weeks, and the goal was to reach the summit in time to watch the sunrise, which meant we needed to start hiking at around four in the morning. I set my alarm for three-thirty, which felt almost cruel at the time. When it went off, I seriously considered cancelling, but I forced myself out of bed, got dressed in the dark, and met my friends at the trailhead. The hike itself was challenging - the path was steep and the darkness made it slightly eerie - but we had torches and kept each other motivated with conversation and laughter. When we finally reached the summit just as the sky began to lighten, the effort felt completely worthwhile. Watching the sun rise over the mountains and valleys below, painting the sky in shades of orange and pink, was one of the most beautiful things I have ever seen. I felt an extraordinary sense of peace and accomplishment standing there with my friends in the early morning silence. It was a reminder that some of the best experiences in life require a little sacrifice and discomfort. I have been on several sunrise hikes since, and I never regret getting up early for them.
