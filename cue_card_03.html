🎤 Sample Speech
✅ Band 7+ Sample Speech (1.5–2 minutes)

The show I would like to talk about is a documentary series called Our Planet, which I have been watching on Netflix over the past few weeks. It is a nature documentary narrated by Sir David Attenborough that explores the extraordinary diversity of life on Earth across different ecosystems - from the frozen Arctic tundra to the depths of the ocean. I watch it about two or three episodes at a time, usually on weekend evenings when I want to unwind. What makes it truly exceptional is the combination of breathtaking cinematography and a deeply moving narrative. The footage of wildlife in their natural habitats is unlike anything I have seen before - there is a sequence involving a walrus colony that I found both heartbreaking and unforgettable. The series also weaves in a powerful environmental message about the impact of climate change on these ecosystems, which I found genuinely thought-provoking. I came away from each episode feeling both inspired by the beauty of the natural world and motivated to make more environmentally conscious choices in my own life. I would wholeheartedly recommend it to anyone who appreciates nature, science, or simply stunning visual storytelling.
