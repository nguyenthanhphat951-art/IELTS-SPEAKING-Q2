Question 1 of 2 Preference / Habit
"Do you like taking pictures of different views?"

✅ Band 7+ Sample Answer

Yes, I love it. Whenever I travel or come across a particularly beautiful view, my first instinct is to reach for my phone or camera. I am especially drawn to natural landscapes - mountain ranges, coastlines, and sunsets. I find that photography encourages me to slow down and really appreciate my surroundings rather than rushing past them. I also enjoy looking back through my photo library and reliving those moments. It is a simple but deeply satisfying hobby.

Question 2 of 2 Preference
"Do you prefer views in urban areas or rural areas?"
🔗 Phrasal Verbs

💡 Collocations

✅ Band 7+ Sample Answer

I think I prefer rural views, honestly. There is something deeply calming about looking out over open countryside, mountains, or the sea - it puts things in perspective and makes the stresses of daily life feel very small. Urban views can be impressive too, especially at night when city lights create a dramatic skyline, but they lack the sense of peace and connection with nature that rural landscapes offer. Whenever I feel overwhelmed, I find that spending time in a natural setting with a beautiful view is the most effective way to recharge.
