💡 Collocations

✅ Band 7+ Sample Answer

My favourite animal is the elephant. I have always been in awe of them - they are incredibly intelligent, deeply social, and display remarkable emotional depth. Elephants are known to mourn their dead, show empathy towards other animals, and have extraordinary memories. I also admire their gentle nature despite their immense size. I had the opportunity to observe elephants in a wildlife sanctuary once, and it was one of the most moving experiences of my life. I am also passionate about elephant conservation, as they face serious threats from habitat loss and poaching.

Question 2 of 2 Past Experience
"Have you ever had a pet before?"
🔗 Phrasal Verbs

💡 Collocations

✅ Band 7+ Sample Answer

Yes, I had a golden retriever named Biscuit when I was growing up. He was with our family for about ten years, and he was an absolute joy. I was responsible for feeding him and taking him for walks every evening, which taught me a lot about responsibility and routine. What I remember most is how unconditionally loving he was - no matter what kind of day I had had, he was always excited to see me. When he passed away, it was genuinely heartbreaking. I would love to have a dog again someday when my lifestyle allows for it.
