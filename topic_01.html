Question 1 of 4 Yes/No + Reason (Personal Habit)
"Do you wear a watch?"
🔗 Phrasal Verbs

💡 Collocations

✅ Band 7+ Sample Answer

Yes, I do wear a watch, actually. I have a simple analogue watch that I put on every morning almost out of habit. Even though my phone can tell the time, I find glancing at my wrist far more convenient, especially when I am in a meeting or a lecture and it would be rude to pull out my phone. I have been wearing watches since I was about twelve, so it just feels strange to leave home without one.

✅ Band 7+ Sample Answer

Yes, I have. When I graduated from high school, my parents gave me a beautiful silver watch as a graduation gift. I still remember opening the box and being completely surprised because I had not expected anything so elegant. They had even had my name engraved on the back, which made it incredibly personal. I still keep it in a drawer and look back on it fondly, even though I do not wear it every day anymore.

Question 3 of 4 Opinion / Reason
"Why do some people wear expensive watches?"
🔗 Phrasal Verbs
show offstand outinvest inlook up to
💡 Collocations
status symbolluxury brandsocial prestigecraftsmanshipinvestment piece
✅ Band 7+ Sample Answer

I think there are a few reasons. For some people, an expensive watch is a status symbol - a way of showing others that they have achieved a certain level of success. Luxury brands like Rolex or Patek Philippe carry enormous social prestige. But for others, it is genuinely about craftsmanship and quality. A fine mechanical watch is a piece of engineering art, and collectors truly appreciate the intricate movements inside. Some people also see high-end watches as investment pieces that hold or even increase in value over time.

Question 4 of 4 Opinion
"Do you think it is important to wear a watch? Why?"
🔗 Phrasal Verbs
keep up withget by withoutdepend oncut down on
💡 Collocations
time managementstay punctualprofessional appearancedigital devicepractical accessory
✅ Band 7+ Sample Answer

Personally, I think it depends on the person and their lifestyle. For professionals who attend back-to-back meetings, wearing a watch helps with time management and gives a polished, professional appearance. However, for younger people who always have their phones nearby, a watch might feel unnecessary. That said, I do believe there is something to be said for the discipline of wearing one - it keeps you more aware of time and helps you stay punctual throughout the day.
