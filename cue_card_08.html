🎤 Sample Speech
✅ Band 7+ Sample Speech (1.5–2 minutes)

I would like to talk about my cousin, Minh, who works as a senior software engineer at VNG Corporation, which is one of Vietnam's most successful and well-known technology companies. VNG operates across multiple sectors, including online gaming, digital payments, and cloud services. Minh joined the company about five years ago after graduating with a degree in computer science, and he has since moved up to a senior position where he leads a small team of developers working on the company's cloud infrastructure. What I find impressive about Minh is his combination of technical expertise and leadership ability. He has told me about the collaborative culture at VNG - how the company invests heavily in employee development, encourages innovation, and gives engineers the freedom to experiment with new ideas. I think VNG's success comes from several factors: its early recognition of Vietnam's growing digital market, its ability to attract and retain talented engineers, and its willingness to diversify into multiple business areas. Minh seems genuinely fulfilled in his role, which I think is a testament to the company's positive work environment. Seeing his career trajectory has inspired me to think more seriously about the kind of company culture I want to work in.
