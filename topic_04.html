Question 1 of 3 Frequency
"How often do you go shopping?"
🔗 Phrasal Verbs
go out forpick upstock up onbrowse around
💡 Collocations
grocery shoppingwindow shoppingonline shoppingshopping spreeweekly shop
✅ Band 7+ Sample Answer

It really depends on what I am shopping for. For groceries, I usually go once or twice a week to stock up on essentials. For clothes or electronics, I shop far less frequently - maybe once every couple of months, and I often browse online first before going to a physical store. I am not really a spontaneous shopper; I tend to plan my purchases in advance rather than going on unplanned shopping sprees.

Question 2 of 3 Preference
"Do you prefer online shopping or in-store shopping?"
🔗 Phrasal Verbs
try oncompare pricesadd to cartreturn items
💡 Collocations
convenienceinstant gratificationproduct qualitydelivery timecustomer service
✅ Band 7+ Sample Answer

I generally prefer online shopping for its sheer convenience. I can compare prices across multiple platforms, read reviews, and have items delivered to my door without leaving home. However, for certain things like clothes or shoes, I still prefer going in-store so I can try them on and check the quality before buying. There is nothing worse than ordering something online and finding it looks completely different in person. So I suppose I use a combination of both depending on what I am buying.

Question 3 of 3 Past Experience
"Have you ever returned anything you bought online?"
🔗 Phrasal Verbs
send backget a refunddeal withfollow up on
💡 Collocations
return policycustomer servicequality issuesize mismatchonline purchase
✅ Band 7+ Sample Answer

Yes, I have, actually. I once ordered a pair of shoes online and when they arrived, the size was completely wrong - they were at least two sizes too small, even though I had ordered my usual size. I contacted the seller, who was very responsive and arranged a return and full refund within a few days. The process was smoother than I expected, which gave me more confidence in online shopping. That said, the experience did remind me to always check the size guide carefully and read customer reviews before buying footwear online.
