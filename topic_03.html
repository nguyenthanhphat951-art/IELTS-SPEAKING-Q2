✅ Band 7+ Sample Answer

I visit quite a variety, actually. On a daily basis, I browse news websites to keep up with current events - I particularly like international news platforms. I also regularly visit educational sites like YouTube for tutorials or Coursera for online courses, since I enjoy learning new skills in my spare time. Occasionally, I will scroll through Reddit to come across interesting discussions on topics I care about, like technology or science.

Whether I want to learn how to fix something around the house or simply unwind with some comedy sketches, I can always find exactly what I am looking for. Question 3 of 3 General Knowledge / Opinion
"What kinds of websites are popular in your country?"
🔗 Phrasal Verbs
log on tocheck outspend time onget hooked on
💡 Collocations
social networking sitee-commerce platformvideo streamingonline gamingdigital news
✅ Band 7+ Sample Answer

In Vietnam, social media platforms like Facebook and TikTok are enormously popular - people use them for everything from keeping in touch with friends to following news and entertainment. E-commerce sites like Shopee and Lazada have also seen explosive growth, as online shopping has become a daily habit for many people. YouTube is widely used for both entertainment and education. And among younger generations, gaming platforms and streaming services like Netflix are increasingly popular. The common thread is convenience - Vietnamese internet users gravitate towards platforms that offer a lot of value in one place.
