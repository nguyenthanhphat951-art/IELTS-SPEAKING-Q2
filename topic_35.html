✅ Band 7+ Sample Answer

Yes, I have a real fondness for my hometown. It is a mid-sized city with a rich cultural heritage and a strong sense of community. What I love most is the food scene - the local street food is incredible and something I genuinely miss whenever I am away. The pace of life is also more relaxed than in the big cities, which I appreciate. If I could change one thing, it would be the traffic - it has gotten considerably worse over the years. But overall, it is a place I am proud to call home.

💡 Collocations

✅ Band 7+ Sample Answer

My hometown is particularly famous for its traditional craft villages and its annual lantern festival. The craft villages have been producing handmade silk and lacquerware for centuries, and they attract both domestic and international tourists. The lantern festival, which takes place every year during the Mid-Autumn Festival, is truly magical - the entire town is illuminated with thousands of colourful lanterns, and the streets fill with music and celebration. It is something I look forward to every year and something I always recommend to visitors.
