🎤 Sample Speech
✅ Band 7+ Sample Speech (1.5–2 minutes)

The job I would consider perfect for me is working as a content strategist or creative director at an international media or technology company. I first became interested in this type of role through my current work in marketing, where I discovered that I am most energised by the intersection of creativity and strategy - developing compelling narratives, understanding audiences, and crafting content that genuinely resonates with people. To reach this level, I know I would need to develop several additional skills. I would need to deepen my expertise in data analytics to better understand audience behaviour, improve my leadership and project management abilities to oversee creative teams, and potentially pursue a postgraduate qualification in communications or business. I would also benefit from gaining experience across different types of media and markets. What makes this job feel perfect for me is that it combines the aspects of work I find most fulfilling - creative problem-solving, storytelling, collaboration, and continuous learning - in a role that has genuine impact. I also value the international dimension; working with diverse teams and audiences from different cultural backgrounds is something I find deeply stimulating. I believe that meaningful work is work that challenges you, allows you to grow, and contributes something of value to the world. This career path, I think, offers all of those things.
