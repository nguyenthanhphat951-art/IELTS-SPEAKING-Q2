✅ Band 7+ Sample Answer

The most recent gift I received was a beautiful leather journal from my best friend on my birthday last month. She knows I love writing and journaling, so she chose something incredibly thoughtful. It had my initials embossed on the cover, which made it feel very personal. I was genuinely touched by how much thought she had put into it. I have already started using it to jot down ideas and reflections, and every time I open it, I think of her.

Question 2 of 2 Self-assessment / Opinion
"Do you think you are good at choosing gifts?"
🔗 Phrasal Verbs
put thought intogo out of one's waysettle forcome up with
💡 Collocations
thoughtful giftpersonal touchgift-givingrecipient's interestslast-minute gift
✅ Band 7+ Sample Answer

I like to think I am reasonably good at it, though I do not always get it right. I try to pay attention to what people mention in conversation - their interests, things they have been wanting, or experiences they would enjoy. I believe the best gifts are ones that show you have been listening. For example, I once got a friend a book by her favourite author that she had not read yet, and she was thrilled. I do sometimes struggle with last-minute gifts, though, and end up settling for something generic, which I always regret.
