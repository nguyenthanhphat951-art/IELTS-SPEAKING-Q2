Question 1 of 3 Frequency
"How often do you watch films?"

💡 Collocations

✅ Band 7+ Sample Answer

I watch films fairly regularly - probably two or three times a week, though mostly at home through streaming platforms like Netflix. I go to the cinema less often, maybe once or twice a month, usually for big blockbuster releases that are worth seeing on the big screen. I am particularly fond of thrillers and science fiction films, so whenever a highly anticipated title comes out, I make a point of catching it in the cinema for the full experience.

Question 2 of 3 Preference
"Do you prefer to watch films at home or in the cinema?"
🔗 Phrasal Verbs
settle inget distractedimmerse oneself inpause and rewind
💡 Collocations
immersive experiencesurround soundcomfortable settingpause buttonhome cinema
✅ Band 7+ Sample Answer

I prefer watching films at home, to be honest. I love being able to pause and rewind if I miss something, and I can watch in comfortable clothes with snacks without worrying about disturbing anyone. The comfort factor is hard to beat. That said, I do appreciate the cinema for certain films - the surround sound and massive screen create an immersive experience that you simply cannot replicate at home. So for epic action films or horror movies, the cinema wins every time.

Question 3 of 3 Preference
"What films do you like?"
🔗 Phrasal Verbs

💡 Collocations

✅ Band 7+ Sample Answer

I am particularly drawn to psychological thrillers and science fiction films. I love films that keep me guessing and make me think long after the credits roll. Christopher Nolan is one of my favourite directors - films like Inception and Interstellar combine stunning visuals with genuinely complex ideas about time, consciousness, and human nature. I also enjoy well-crafted dramas that explore human relationships in depth. What I look for in any film is strong character development and a story that feels authentic and meaningful.
